Lisbon

Lisbon is Portugal’s hilly, coastal capital city. From imposing São Jorge Castle, the view encompasses the old city’s pastel-colored buildings, Tagus Estuary and Ponte 25 de Abril suspension bridge. Nearby, the National Azulejo Museum displays 5 centuries of decorative ceramic tiles. Just outside Lisbon is a string of Atlantic beaches, from Cascais to Estoril.

At a glance

A holiday in Lisbon is truly a city break that has it all! Travellers get to explore the city's seven hills (exploring by tram is an inexpensive and truly marvellous way of doing so), numerous beaches and they get to enjoy the true taste of Portuguese grilled fish, nata pastries and its "green" wine. Furthermore, Booterstown Travel offers three magnificent hotel choices, personally handpicked for their excellent value for money and proximity to the city's main attractions. Attractions

Lisbon BTT city breaks always begin at the Lisbon Cathedral (left image above, mark A on map below). This is not only because of the famous landmark but also as the area contains the terminal for the city's emblematic trams, an easy and inexpensive way to enjoy all that Lisbon has to offer. Other unmissable attractions include the views from the top of Santa Justa (centre image above, mark B on map below) as well as the beautiful Rua Augusta Arch dating back to the Roman times (right image, mark C on the map).
