About Booterstown Travel

Following years of travel and endless stories to recount, Anna and Dimitri thought that other people would want to experience the wonderful cities of Europe like a true local. Hence, Booterstown Travel was born in 2021, offering an elegant, safe and truly local way of travel and of experiencing the Continent's best cities. Whether you are into culture, food or just want to relax and get pampered in one of our handpicked hotels we offer the very best in "City Break" travel!

Read our reviews on social media, ask about our price promise and either book directly through our website or send us your enquiry filling in the form below and we will do the rest...Let's travel!
